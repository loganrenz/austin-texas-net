## Austin Neighborhoods

Austin is a **city of neighborhoods**, each with its own distinct character, price point, and lifestyle. From the hip boutiques of South Congress to the family-friendly streets of Mueller to the suburban sprawl of Cedar Park, understanding Austin's geography is the key to finding your place in the city.

## Central Austin

**Downtown** is all business during the day and all nightlife after dark. **South Congress (SoCo)** is walkable, artsy, and expensive. **East Austin** is the creative hub with studios, breweries, and a rapidly changing demographic. **Hyde Park** offers a classic college-town vibe with historic homes.

## North Austin

**Mueller** is Austin's model planned community with parks, local shops, and an increasingly vibrant food scene. **The Domain** is a master-planned live-work-play district anchored by tech offices and upscale retail. **Cedar Park** and **Round Rock** offer suburban living with excellent schools and lower costs.

## South & West

**Zilker** and **Barton Hills** put you steps from the greenbelt and Barton Springs. **South Lamar** is a corridor of restaurants, bars, and local businesses. **Bee Cave** and **Lakeway** offer Hill Country charm with lake access.
